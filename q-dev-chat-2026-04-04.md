## Can we build our own Google Meet inside Ratibu?

**Yes — and it's very achievable.** Here's the honest breakdown:

### What "Google Meet" actually is at its core
It's WebRTC — a browser/app standard for peer-to-peer audio/video. The hard part Google solved is the **signaling server** (coordinating connections) and **TURN/STUN servers** (handling NAT traversal when peers can't connect directly).
